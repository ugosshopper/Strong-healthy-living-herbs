## Herb Name
Bitter Leaf

## Local Names
- Hausa: Shiwaka  
- Yoruba: Ewuro  
- Igbo: Onugbu  

## Scientific Name
Vernonia amygdalina

## Health Benefits
Bitter leaf helps with:
- Blood sugar balance
- Liver support
- Digestive health
- Immune boosting
- Detoxification

## How to Prepare
- Wash leaves thoroughly.
- Boil and drink as tea or use in soup.

## Dosage
- 1 cup of tea once daily

## Safety
- Avoid excessive intake during pregnancy.
