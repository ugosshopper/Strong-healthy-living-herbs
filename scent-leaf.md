## Herb Name
Scent Leaf

## Local Names
- Igbo: Nchuanwu  
- Yoruba: Efinrin  
- Hausa: Daidoya  

## Scientific Name
Ocimum gratissimum

## Health Benefits
Scent leaf supports:
- Digestive health
- Blood sugar regulation
- Respiratory health
- Immune support
- Antimicrobial action

## How to Prepare
- Boil fresh leaves and drink as tea.
- Can be used in cooking.

## Dosage
- 1 cup tea once daily

## Safety
- Safe in moderate amounts.
